The timelines are accelerating.

The point is not to worship a date. The point is to notice when capability, deployment, markets, and social impact are moving faster than institutions can adapt.

The argument is no longer that the singularity is someday.

Software disrupted travel. Platforms disrupted taxis. AI is now moving into tax advisory, translation, booking, coding, research, education, and professional judgment. The planning problem is what families, workers, companies, and governments do when no one has a stable map anymore.
